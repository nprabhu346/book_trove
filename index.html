The Story Behind the Stories

BOOK TROVE brings together carefully curated pre-loved books that inspire imagination and create memories. We believe every book deserves a second home and every child deserves a shelf full of wonders.

Our mission is to make high-quality literature accessible and sustainable. By choosing pre-loved, you're not just saving a book, you're joining a movement of conscious readers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the condition of the books? expand_more
Every book undergoes a thorough quality check. We only stock books that are in excellent to very good condition, ensuring a premium reading experience for your child.
How does shipping work? expand_more
We ship PAN India via reliable courier partners. Shipping is usually dispatched within 24-48 hours of order confirmation.
How do I place an order? expand_more
It's simple! Just browse our collection, take a screenshot or note the book names, and click the 'WhatsApp' button to message us. We'll confirm availability and send payment details.
What age groups do you cater to? expand_more
We specialize in children's books ranging from birth (0 years) up to early teens (13 years), including board books, picture books, and chapter series.
Are the books sanitized? expand_more
Yes, hygiene is our priority. Every pre-loved book is cleaned and sanitized before being packaged for its new home.
